Michael Jackson

The King of Pop

Michael Jackson was born on August 29th, 1958. He was the 8th child born in the the illustrious Jackson Family. He his professional debut at the age of 6 along with his brothers to comprise the famous Jackson 5.

Jackson went solo by age 13, releasing several albums. His solo career began to pick up steam with his release of Off-The-Wall, which was a departure from his earlier music that had a motown tune to it. His next album would be his best selling, and - till this day - the best selling album in the world: Thriller.
